About Me

Hello! I'm Simsek Sua Tortu, a professional photographer specializing in landscape and portrait photography. With over 10 years of experience capturing moments and emotions, I've developed a keen eye for detail and a distinctive style that combines natural light with creative compositions.

My journey with photography began when I was just a teenager exploring the natural wonders around my hometown. What started as a hobby quickly transformed into a passion that has taken me around the world, from the majestic mountains of Patagonia to the serene lakes of Scandinavia.

I believe that every image tells a story, and my goal is to create photographs that not only capture a moment in time but also evoke emotion and connection. Whether I'm shooting breathtaking landscapes or intimate portraits, I approach each session with creativity, technical precision, and a genuine love for my craft.
